Election Calculator

Step 1: Import Data

A simple example is already loaded. The names of candidates are listed at the top, and the votes are listed below, with a separator of "~~~" between the sections. Each vote lists a preference for each candidate. "1" is the first preference, "2" is the second preference, and so on. "0" indicates the lack of a preference. Make sure every vote has a number for all the candidates. Edit the candidates and votes as necessary, and press the "Import Data" button to confirm before continuing.
